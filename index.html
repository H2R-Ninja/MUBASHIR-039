What is Photography?

Photography is the art of capturing light to create images. Whether it's a stunning landscape or a candid street shot, every photo tells a story. I discovered this hobby when I was twelve years old and haven't put the camera down since.

Why I Love It

Photography teaches me to slow down and notice the beauty in everyday things. It's also a great way to express creativity without words. Each click of the shutter is a tiny adventure. Seeing the world through a lens changes everything.
